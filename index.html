The Mynd Tree

The Mynd Tree is an educational and psychological counseling centre for adults and children with mental health issues and learning disabilities. Located in Tirupur, Tamil Nadu but available nationwide through online meetings, we provide psychological counseling and therapy and mental illness, for slow learners, for learning disabilities.

We take a non-judgemental approach, for we have great appreciation for the dignity and worth of every individual. Mynd Tree is a physically and emotionally safe, confidential and respectful environment. We are always open to listen to every individual irrespective of age, gender, culture, spirituality, race, and religion.
