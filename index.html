Mynd Tree

Mynd Tree Learning Centre is an educational and psychological counseling centre for children and adults with mental health issues and learning disabilities. Located in Tirupur, Tamil Nadu but available nationwide through online meetings, we provide remedial therapy for slow learners to get back on track in school and psychological counseling for those with mental illnesses.

We take a non-judgemental approach, for we have great appreciation for the dignity and worth of every individual. Mynd Tree is a physically and emotionally safe, confidential, and respectful environment. We are always open to listen to every individual irrespective of age, gender, culture, spirituality, race, and religion.
