When I completed my masters in psychology I was looking for a unique field where I could make a difference. Just then I came across a special school who were hiring teachers to teach children with learning disabilities and slow learners and scholastically Backward.I worked there and then the work became a passion. I could see the difference I was making in lives of children and their parents. These children were drop outs from mainstream as they could not cope up with the syllabus. After that I started my own Mynd Tree Learning center and since then there has been no looking back.

I approached schools and gave an orientation of my work and how it would be helpful to children, teachers and the school. The teachers don't have to take extra burden which gets shared by me and at the same time the child is also being benefited with specialized training. This in turn starts boosting their self esteem and self confidence. I try to bring out the joy of learning and aim to reach as many as I can. Meanwhile I also started learning other modalities of psychotherapy and started working in the area of individual therapy. Mynd Tree now is a Psychological and Educational counseling centre with a tie up and network with various experienced groups of Psychologist, Counselors and Educational Consultants.
