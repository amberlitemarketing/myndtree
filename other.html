Cognitive Behavioural Therapy

Cognitive behavioural therapy is a form of therapy dedicated to altering dysfunctional emotions and behaviors that impede learning, wellbeing, and day-to-day life. For both children and adults, this method of behavior modification improves self-esteem and life skills through discussion, coping mechanisms, and therapeutic activities.
