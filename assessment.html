Assessment

Our assessment identifies which cognitive abilities may be impaired or need the most development for students with possible learning disabilities.

A 2016 Na help.

For those in need of remedial therapy, identifying that a child’s struggle in schoolwork is not due to lack of dedication or studying, but to an impairment beyond their control, is the first step. Bringing them to licensed professionals, such as those at Mynd Tree Centre, is the next. From there, a personalized assessment is performed to gauge a more accurate overview of the student’s abilities and areas of concern.

This assessment explores various areas of knowledge, including both audio and visual memory, discrimination, and retention, as well mathematical subjects like number ccancellation and general learning skills like attention span. The assessment continues based on the performance of the student; topics that are evidently not the source of perplexity are not dwelled upon, while trouble spots are given careful attention to understand the extent of difficulty.

Proper assessment is generally a necessity for understanding the roots of learning difficulties. Constant mistakes in spelling can be a result of dyslexia, dysgraphia, or something else entirely; problems in multiplication can be caused by dyscalculia or more indirectly related to ADHD. To completely address these struggles, we need a precise understanding of the disabilities behind them.
